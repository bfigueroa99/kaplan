    \item When to use the Infinitive (to + verb)
    \item Common verbs that follow each pattern

\end{itemize}

\section{Key Concepts: Verb Patterns}

In English, when one verb follows another, the second verb changes its form.

\begin{vocabbox}[title=Pattern 1: Verb + -ing (Gerund)]
Use \textbf{-ing} after these verbs:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Enjoy:} I enjoy \textbf{reading}. \trans{Disfrutar}
    \item \textbf{Avoid:} She avoids \textbf{driving} at night. \trans{Evitar}
    \item \textbf{Finish:} He finished \textbf{working}. \trans{Terminar}
    \item \textbf{Suggest:} I suggest \textbf{going} by bus. \trans{Sugerir}
    \item \textbf{Recommend:} They recommend \textbf{booking} early. \trans{Recomendar}
    \item \textbf{Consider:} We considered \textbf{moving}. \trans{Considerar}
\end{itemize}
\end{vocabbox}

\begin{vocabbox}[title=Pattern 2: Verb + to + Infinitive]
Use \textbf{to + verb} after these verbs:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Want:} I want \textbf{to sleep}. \trans{Querer}
    \item \textbf{Hope:} I hope \textbf{to see} you. \trans{Esperar (deseo)}
    \item \textbf{Decide:} She decided \textbf{to leave}. \trans{Decidir}
    \item \textbf{Plan:} We plan \textbf{to visit}. \trans{Planear}
    \item \textbf{Promise:} He promised \textbf{to help}. \trans{Prometer}
    \item \textbf{Refuse:} They refused \textbf{to pay}. \trans{Rehusar}
    \item \textbf{Learn:} I learned \textbf{to swim}. \trans{Aprender}
\end{itemize}
\end{vocabbox}

\section{Grammar Focus: Common Mistakes}

\begin{grammarbox}[title=Watch Out!]
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Incorrect:} I enjoy to play football.
    \item \textbf{Correct:} I enjoy \textbf{playing} football.
    \item \textbf{Incorrect:} I want going home.
    \item \textbf{Correct:} I want \textbf{to go} home.
\end{itemize}
\end{grammarbox}

\section{Key Takeaways}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Memorize which verbs take \textbf{-ing} (enjoy, avoid, suggest).
    \item Memorize which verbs take \textbf{to + infinitive} (want, hope, decide).
    \item \textbf{Recommend} and \textbf{Suggest} are followed by -ing (I recommend visiting...).
\end{itemize}
