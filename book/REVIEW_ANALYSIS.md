**‚úÖ STRENGTHS:**
1. Clear progression from basic to advanced tenses
2. Each chapter has consistent structure (Objectives ‚Üí Reading ‚Üí Grammar ‚Üí Exercises)

5. Good use of tables and visual organization

**‚ö†Ô∏è ISSUES IDENTIFIED:**

1. **Jump from Chapter 3 to Chapter 8:**

   - Chapter 5 (Business Vocab) appears early but grouped in Part VII

   - Chapter 11 dedicated to time expressions

   - May be redundant

   - British English specifics relegated to end (Part XI)
   - Should be woven throughout all chapters
   - Missed opportunity for cultural learning

5. **Part IX Separation:**
   - Narrative skills separated from Past Narratives (Part V)
   - Could be consolidated

   - chapter10_new.tex and chapter11_new.tex exist alongside regular numbering
